A building design is a setup consistent with that a home or another building is constructed. This includes each of the interior and exterior of the structure. Formal building design plans, usually called blueprints, are typically created by architects and should often be filed with native governmental agencies before construction can lawfully begin.

Many professionals are ordinarily concerned with building design. This includes architects, who commonly draw up the plans, in addition to plumbers, HVAC professionals, and electricians. advanced buildings, corresponding to high-rise flats or business buildings, might also need the services of an engineer. A surveyor also could be required to judge the plot of land on which the structure is meant to be built, and a designer would possibly develop an idea for the encompassing property. The main purpose of surveying in engineering is to see the three-dimensional relationships between totally different locations.
